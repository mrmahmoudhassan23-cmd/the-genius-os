# Naming Conventions

This document defines the official naming standards across The Genius OS.

---

## 1. Tasks
**Format:**
`[AREA]-[TYPE]-[NUMBER]-[SHORT_DESCRIPTION]`

**Examples:**
- TEACH-PLAN-001-Fractions_Introduction
- STUDY-REV-014-Midterm_Review

---

## 2. Lessons
**Format:**
`Subject_Grade_Topic_Sequence`

**Examples:**
- Math_Grade7_Fractions_01
- Math_Grade8_Algebra_Intro

---

## 3. Students
**Format:**
`FirstName_LastName_ID`

**Examples:**
- Ahmed_Ali_1023
- Sara_Mohamed_2041

---

## 4. Content
**Format:**
`TYPE_TOPIC_LEVEL_VERSION`

**Examples:**
- VIDEO_Fractions_Basic_v1
- PDF_Algebra_Intermediate_v2

---

## 5. Projects
**Format:**
`PROJECT_NAME_PHASE_STATUS`

**Examples:**
- GeniusOS_CoreFoundation_Active
- EBIS_Integration_Planning

---

## 6. Documents
**Format:**
`DOC_Category_Title_Date`

**Examples:**
- DOC_LessonPlan_Fractions_2026-07
- DOC_Review_Report_June2026

---

## 7. Media
**Format:**
`TYPE_Subject_Topic_Audience`

**Examples:**
- VIDEO_Math_Fractions_Students
- IMAGE_Algebra_Chart_Teaching
