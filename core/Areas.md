# Areas

This document defines the official Areas used across The Genius OS.

---

## 1. Teaching Area
**Purpose:**
Manage all teaching-related operations and classroom execution.

**Responsibilities:**
- Deliver lessons
- Execute lesson plans
- Manage student engagement during sessions

**Examples:**
- Live math lesson
- Group tutoring session
- Exam preparation class

---

## 2. Planning Area
**Purpose:**
Structure and design educational content and lesson flow.

**Responsibilities:**
- Create lesson plans
- Define learning objectives
- Organize curriculum structure

**Examples:**
- Weekly lesson plan
- Monthly curriculum roadmap
- Topic breakdown sheet

---

## 3. Content Area
**Purpose:**
Handle creation and management of educational materials.

**Responsibilities:**
- Produce learning content
- Maintain content library
- Structure multimedia resources

**Examples:**
- Video explanation
- PDF worksheets
- Practice question banks

---

## 4. Student Area
**Purpose:**
Manage all student-related academic tracking and records.

**Responsibilities:**
- Track performance
- Maintain student profiles
- Monitor progress and attendance

**Examples:**
- Student progress report
- Attendance sheet
- Performance dashboard

---

## 5. Analytics Area
**Purpose:**
Analyze system performance and educational outcomes.

**Responsibilities:**
- Generate insights
- Track KPIs
- Evaluate teaching effectiveness

**Examples:**
- Monthly performance report
- Student success metrics
- Content engagement analytics
